About Future University

Future University Bareilly is a leading educational institution in Uttar Pradesh, dedicated to academic excellence and innovation. Located in Faridpur, Bareilly, we offer cutting-edge programs designed to prepare students for the challenges of tomorrow.

Our Vision

To become a global hub for innovation, employability, and societal impact by bridging academia and industry needs.

Leadership

Under the guidance of Mr. Mukesh gupta (chancalor) and Mr. M K Singh (Principal), we are committed to nurturing the next generation of leaders and innovators.
