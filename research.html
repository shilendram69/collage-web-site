World-Class Research Facilities

Our state-of-the-art laboratories and research centers provide the tools and environment needed for groundbreaking discoveries.

Advanced Materials Research Lab

Equipped with XRD, SEM, and TEM for nanotechnology and material science research with applications in energy storage and construction.

Center for Genomics and Bioinformatics

High-throughput sequencing and computational resources for agricultural and medical genomics research.

Cognitive Science Laboratory

EEG, eye-tracking, and VR systems for studying human cognition, learning, and AI-human interaction.

Sustainable Development Research Center

Interdisciplinary hub for research on sustainable urban planning, water management, and rural development.

Robotics and Automation Lab

Developing robotic solutions for manufacturing, agriculture, and healthcare applications.

High Performance Computing Center

Petascale computing resources for complex simulations and big data analytics across disciplines.
